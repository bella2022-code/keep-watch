# Keep Watch

> *Under quiet, gentle observation.*

A pixel-art focus tool built around the feeling of being **gently watched while you work**. Not pressured. Not gamified. Just quietly accompanied.

---

## What This Is

Keep Watch is an immersive focus tool that operates as a small pixel-world. Instead of a productivity app with timers and streaks, it's a living world where a quietly attentive Officer watches you work—not as a taskmaster, but as someone who just hasn't clocked out yet.

The user enters a small world. The world is empty most of the time. Occasionally, characters pass through. When you finish focusing, one of them hands you a card with a quiet word of encouragement.

That's the whole product.

---

## Core Philosophy

| Principle | What it means |
|---|---|
| **No UI is the UI** | The card *is* the time picker. Tapping the character *is* Start. There are no buttons. |
| **Cumulative, not streak** | Total days only. Never resets. No daily punishment. |
| **Gentle supervision** | Officer is in uniform but languid. Watching, not pressuring. |
| **World first** | The home screen is a scene, not a tool. |
| **Low-interruption immersion** | Minimal sound. Companions pass quietly. No strong alerts. |
| **Contrast as soul** | Military uniform + relaxed body. Strict discipline + pink hearts. |
