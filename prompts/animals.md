## Maker Self-Portrait Template

```

[hair color and style: e.g., short black hair / long brown hair / bun],
[skin tone],
[facial features: e.g., round glasses / no glasses, gentle smile],
[clothing: e.g., simple sweater / hoodie / button-up shirt],
[clothing color],
gentle relaxed expression, slightly tilted head,
4 facing variations: left, right, front, back,
restricted 8-color palette,

1px outline #2A2128, no anti-aliasing
```
